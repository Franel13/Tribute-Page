Ayrton Senna

"The São Paulo taxi driver"

Here's a time line of Ayrton Senna's life:

  • 1960 - Senna was born in the Pro-Matre Maternity Hospital of Santana, a neighbourhood of São Paulo.
  • 1973 - Begins racing in go-karts.
  • 1977 - Wins the South American Kart Championship.
  • 1981 - Moves to England and wins RAC and Townsend-Thoreson Formula Ford 1600 Championships.
  • 1982 - Wins British and European Formula Ford 2000 championships.
  • 1984 - First podium in Formula One with Toleman Motorsport.
  • 1985 - Signs with Lotus and gets the first 'Grand Slam'.
  • 1988 - Signs with McLaren and wins his first Formula One world championship title.
  • 1991 - Becomes the the youngest ever three-time world champion.
  • 1994 - Dies due to an accident on the track of San Marino Grand Prix aged 34.
